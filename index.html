About Us

Welcome to Sri Laxmi Electrical Hardware Sanitary & Paints

We are a leading provider of electrical supplies, hardware items, sanitary products, and paints in Khammam. With over two decades of experience, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the industry.

Our mission is to provide high-quality products at competitive prices, backed by exceptional customer service. We take pride in offering expert advice and solutions tailored to meet your specific requirements.

Whether you're a contractor, builder, or a homeowner looking for quality products for your project, we have everything you need under one roof.

Visit our store at Khammam - Yellandu Road, and experience the difference of shopping with a company that values quality and customer satisfaction above all.
